About Me

Learn more about me and my journey!

Since high school I have been into cars. What started out as an innocent curiosity has evolved into an expensive obssesion. I love to tinker and take things apart to figure out how they work. I get a sense of satisfaction when something isn't working and I replace it and it somehow functions again.

I have owned various cars, some good some not so good. I've upgraded suspension, replaced shot ball joints, and somehow, through the grace of god, successfully replaced timing belts.

In recent years, thanks to my good friend Max, I have developed a passion for motorsports. Supporting my favorite team, McLaren Racing, we watch Formula 1 every race weekend. Recently I have gotten into watching the European Le Mans series as well.

One of my other hobbies is sim racing. Repping the iconic Logitech G920 I play popular sim racing games such as Assetto Corsa, Assetto Corsa Compitizione, and the F1 games. It's much cheaper to crash a virtual car than a real car.
